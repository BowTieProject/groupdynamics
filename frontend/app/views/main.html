FAQ

How do people use groupstats?

Let me give you some examples: You can us groupstats.io to analyze how the relationship with your partner is evolving over time. You can analyze group chats to see who talks the most. You can identify who is most emotional in chats... and much much more.

With which devices can I use this service?

You can export WhatsApp chats with all mobile devices. You can upload chats with Android or your desktop computer. You can then share and view stats on all devices, including desktops.

How is my data stored?

We don't store your chats. When you upload your chat, we analyze it and then drop the data. We keep the meta data so you are able to easily share your stats.
